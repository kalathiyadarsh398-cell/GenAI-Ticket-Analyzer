# Billing and Subscription Policy

Rules on how subscriptions, billing cycles, invoices, and double-charges are handled.

## Subscriptions and Billing Cycle
- Subscriptions auto-renew monthly on the exact calendar day the account was upgraded.
- Customers can cancel their subscription at any time via the billing tab. Access remains until the billing cycle ends.

## Double Charges and Payment Errors
- If a customer reports a double charge or payment failure error:
  1. Look up the transaction ID in the billing system.
  2. If a duplicate charge is found, reverse the duplicate transaction.
  3. Refunds for reversed charges take **2 to 3 business days** to return to the customer's account.

## Invoices and Receipts
- Invoices are automatically emailed to the account holder on the billing date.
- Past invoices can be downloaded as PDFs from the billing settings page.
