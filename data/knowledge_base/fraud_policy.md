# Fraud Prevention and Phishing Policy

Procedures for handling identity theft, stolen credit cards, and security breaches.

## Stolen Cards and Unauthorized Purchases
- If a customer reports a stolen credit card was used to make unauthorized purchases:
  1. Lock the corresponding user account immediately to prevent further activity.
  2. Cancel any pending orders associated with the stolen card.
  3. Advise the cardholder to file a chargeback with their bank and report the theft.

## Suspicious Phishing Emails
- If a customer reports receiving a suspicious email claiming to be from our company:
  1. Do not click any links or download attachments.
  2. Forward the email headers to `security@companysupport.com`.
  3. We never ask for full passwords, credit card numbers, or 2FA codes via email.
