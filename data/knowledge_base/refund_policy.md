# Refund Policy

Our company refund rules help customer support determine eligibility and resolve dispute claims.

## Eligibility Window
- Customers can request a full refund within **30 days** of the original purchase date.
- Product must be in its original packaging, unused, and in the same condition as received.

## Refund Processing
- Refunds are processed back to the customer's original payment method.
- Standard processing time is **5 to 7 business days** for the funds to reflect in the bank account.

## Exceptions & Non-Refundable Items
- Custom-made or personalized items are not eligible for returns or refunds.
- Software licenses, digital downloads, and gift cards are strictly non-refundable.
- Standard shipping fees are non-refundable unless the item arrived damaged or defective.
