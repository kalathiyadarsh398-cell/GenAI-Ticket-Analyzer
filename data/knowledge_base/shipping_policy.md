# Shipping and Delivery Policy

We strive to ship orders quickly and keep customers updated on their deliveries.

## Shipping Timeline
- **Domestic Shipping**: Standard delivery takes **3 to 5 business days**. Express shipping takes **1 to 2 business days**.
- **International Shipping**: Standard delivery takes **7 to 14 business days** depending on customs processes.

## Order Tracking
- A tracking number is sent via email within **24 hours** after the package leaves our warehouse.
- Customers can track their packages on our website dashboard using their Order ID.

## Delayed or Lost Packages
- If an order is delayed by more than **3 business days** beyond the estimated delivery date, contact support.
- If a package is confirmed lost in transit by the carrier, we will ship a replacement free of charge or issue a full refund.
