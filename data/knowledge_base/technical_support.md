# Technical Support Troubleshooting Policy

Guidelines for resolving application errors, syncing bugs, and crashes.

## App Crashes and Settings Tab Issues
- If the application crashes when opening the settings tab, it is a known bug. Steps to resolve:
  1. Go to settings on your mobile device, find the application, and clear cache/data.
  2. Uninstall the app, restart the device, and reinstall the latest version from the app store.
  3. If the issue persists, collect the device OS version and send a log report.

## Data Sync Failures
- If the dashboard displays a spinning wheel or fails to load data:
  1. Verify the device is connected to a stable internet connection.
  2. Log out of the app and log back in to force a token refresh.
  3. Ensure background app refresh is enabled in the device system settings.
